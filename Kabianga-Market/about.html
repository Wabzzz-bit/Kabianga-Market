About Kabianga University Market

We are a student-driven platform where Kabianga University students can buy, sell, and exchange items within the campus community. Our goal is to promote convenience, entrepreneurship, and sustainability through local trade.

From clothes and shoes to food, perfumes, and accessories — we’ve got something for everyone!
